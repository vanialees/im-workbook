WEEK 7
In this week's class, we were introduced to the use of Arduino - linking an Arduino with Arduino IDE and looking through Tinkercad to get an easier understanding of working with Arduino. We started experimenting physical computing with Arduino, coding blinking LEDs and using ultrasonic sensors. As we were experimenting with the blinking LED lights, I immediately thought of the Pulse Room which I find highly interesting because of its combination of heartbeat sensor and light. One thing I found unique of this installation is how it differs based on the viewers' cardiac rhythm - therefore, making it "personalized" to each person. I found the entire concept of the Pulse Room to be simple yet very creative.
