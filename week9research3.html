I was really inspired by the BSTD website because of how it turns digital interaction into an immersive, artistic experience. From the moment I landed on the site, the bold typography, fluid animations, and moody sound design completely pulled me in. It doesn’t just showcase work—it feels like the work is the experience. I love how it blurs the line between interface and art, reminding me that web design can be just as emotional and expressive as physical installations. It made me think differently about how I approach interactive media—less like a layout, more like a living composition.
