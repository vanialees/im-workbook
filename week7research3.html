Click picture to open SIDLEE

Another one of my inspiration is SIDLEE's website where it generatively changes images for the first few seconds when you open the website. It automatically changes into a different screen that is layed out as a general online shop page. I think that the automatic changes and constantly moving makes the website really unique. I also noticed that in the website, with the hover effect, the words that follow the cursor changes based on what you are hovering. I took inspiration for my website when the cursor is hovering certain parts of my website, it has small "notes" for the viewer - for instance, the "go to next page" or "go back".
